[Sovigaz—Welding Electrode Industrial Gas Joint Stock Company](https://sovigaz.com.vn/) is a state-owned enterprise leading the production of medical gases, industrial gases, medical gases, welding electrodes, and chemicals in Vietnam. Over more than 45 years of operation, Sovigaz has become one of Vietnam’s leading manufacturers of industrial gases, welding electrodes, and chemicals, and a trusted brand across industries with the noble mission of “serving people’s livelihoods” and acting responsibly toward society.

![](../../../assets/blog/sovigaz-nang-cao-nang-luc-thuc-thi-muc-tieu-simplamo/Hinh-anh-casestudy-85-1024x576.png)

## 1. Sovigaz and the desire to improve goal execution capability

Throughout its journey, Sovigaz has operated with “People” as the center of all activities, connecting the interests of employees and the company so they can grow together. Accordingly, achieving the annual business plan depends heavily on the team’s **goal execution capability**. Mr. Phong had also searched for many different methods and tools to help the team share the same way of breaking down, tracking, and executing goals better, but had not yet found a method that was suitable and simple enough.

Below are some of his and the team’s expectations when they came to Simplamo:

- A **scientific and suitable way to break down goals** for Sovigaz’s current operating structure, helping unleash the strength of each member and emphasizing their contribution to the organization’s success.
- An effective **method for coordinating execution** of business strategy across departments, ensuring that everything always serves the common goal.
- A **way to maintain execution** regularly and consistently across the team, so leadership can easily understand everything happening in the organization.

![](../../../assets/blog/sovigaz-nang-cao-nang-luc-thuc-thi-muc-tieu-simplamo/Hinh-anh-casestudy--1024x576.png)

## 2. Simplamo—a management software focused on people, improving execution capability for Sovigaz

Strengthening Sovigaz’s internal capability is the foundation for the company to achieve its 2023 business goals, overcome economic challenges, and grow strongly in the coming years. When approaching Simplamo, Mr. Phong realized that it was not merely a management software; behind it was a scientific goal-management mindset focused on people, helping the team complete the business plan exactly as he expected.

On June 13, 2023, Sovigaz decided to implement Simplamo. With guidance and companionship from Simplamo experts, Sovigaz achieved strong progress after only three months of adoption:

![](../../../assets/blog/sovigaz-nang-cao-nang-luc-thuc-thi-muc-tieu-simplamo/Hinh-anh-casestudy-88-1024x576.png)

- **Strengthening a solid people foundation by building an accountability chart**

Clarifying Sovigaz’s organizational structure and the five main roles at each position provides a shared and transparent perspective across the team, highlighting each individual’s value and contribution to the company’s bigger picture. When members sit in the right seats and take on the right roles, it creates the basis for accurate goal decomposition.

- **Helping the team become familiar with a new quarterly goal-setting approach that is simple and scientific**

Simplamo’s goal-setting mindset focuses on being **easy to understand, easy to measure, and easy to remember**. Each goal is assigned to a specific person, aligned with their position on the accountability chart. Each goal is broken down into important milestones, and the entire team understands the progress of each goal, ensuring a shared perspective for effective coordination in execution.

Finally, company-level quarterly goals are limited to fewer than seven, and each individual has at most three goals to achieve the highest focus and completion capability.

- **Organizing smooth weekly meetings that closely follow goals every week**

After agreeing on the list of Q3 goals, the Sovigaz team became familiar with Simplamo’s seven-step weekly meeting rhythm and increasingly enjoyed this meeting framework. This performance meeting framework helps Sovigaz focus on what needs to be reviewed each week (goals, metrics, to-dos), provides tools to measure execution, solve core issues, and automatically generate reports. All discussions are extremely open and full of enthusiasm, with members sharing from a common perspective.

- **Organizing the Q4 meeting and successfully building Q4 goals**

At the beginning of October, after a quarter of applying Simplamo and becoming familiar with setting, breaking down, and closely following execution, the Sovigaz team held its Q4 meeting. There, the team summarized Q3 goals and continued to build Q4 goals. Accordingly, the Q4 plan was built very tightly, linked from the company level down to each department, ensuring high achievability and strong commitment from Sovigaz’s leadership.
