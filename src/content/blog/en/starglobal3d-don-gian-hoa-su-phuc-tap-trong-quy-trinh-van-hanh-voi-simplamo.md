**[StarGlobal3D](https://starglobal3d.com)** is one of the pioneering companies in 3D digitalization solutions, granted an exclusive patent by the **United States Patent and Trademark Office (USPTO)** for its “smart 3D/360 interactive Web/App” product.

**Mr. Tran Duy Hao – Founder of StarGlobal 3D** is a leader with a technical background and deep professional expertise. After working for large corporations and multinational companies, he also developed a strong understanding of business operating processes.

![Starglobal3D decides to implement Simplamo](../../../assets/blog/starglobal3d-don-gian-hoa-su-phuc-tap-trong-quy-trinh-van-hanh-voi-simplamo/Hinh-anh-casestudy-6.png)

With the goal of strong growth and expansion into international markets such as the U.S. and Canada, Mr. Hao applied systematic operating processes to help the business develop sustainably and pursue its business goals. He had used tools such as Excel, Base, MyXTeam, and Amis, but after a period of implementation he found that these tools made the process very **complex**, put pressure on the team, and were hard to follow. In the end, the **goals** that had been set did not achieve the **results** initially expected.

## 1. The challenges StarGlobal 3D faced when using complex operating processes

Using **complex operating processes** made the StarGlobal 3D team **unable to execute goals** according to plan, even though Mr. Hao had very clear visions and goals. Some of the difficulties he faced included:

- The team did not execute according to strategy.
- There was no operating process simple enough for the team to follow easily.
- The software being used only managed daily tasks; the team did not focus on what was important.
- There was no timely synchronization or collaboration across departments.
- Planning was based on intuition, without a clear framework or a business-wide picture for evaluation and goal tracking.
- There was no feedback from the team during execution.

![Starglobal3D implements Simplamo](../../../assets/blog/starglobal3d-don-gian-hoa-su-phuc-tap-trong-quy-trinh-van-hanh-voi-simplamo/Hinh-anh-casestudy-8.png)

To untangle the business at that time, Mr. Hao needed a management software that was **simple**, **right-sized** for the organization, and able to create a solid foundation so the team could understand **his vision** and **closely follow** goal execution. As someone who already had a systematic operating mindset and a high level of discipline, what he lacked at that moment was the “right” tool to move the business forward.

## 2. Simplamo helps Starglobal 3D simplify complex operations and stay close to 2023 goal execution

Mr. Tran Duy Hao built Starglobal3D around the values of continuous development and high expectations for what the company creates. He spent a great deal of time learning about Simplamo and decided to use it in January 2023. He shared: “I hope that using Simplamo, with its simplicity, will help Starglobal 3D build a simple operating system, manage goals effectively, achieve the expectations we have in business and even more — as Simplamo’s slogan promises — Simple and more.”

![Starglobal3D implements Simplamo](../../../assets/blog/starglobal3d-don-gian-hoa-su-phuc-tap-trong-quy-trinh-van-hanh-voi-simplamo/Hinh-anh-casestudy-7.png)

StarGlobal 3D is also a startup that successfully raised funding on “Shark Tank Vietnam – Season 4” with investment from Shark Pham Thanh Hung of CEN GROUP.

According to Ms. Nguyen Thi Nghia – Simplamo expert: “To plan and execute successfully, we need to identify the work in the present while still aiming toward the company’s long-term vision. That means we move both near and far: the team works on current activities while still moving toward future goals. Building an **annual business plan** is one **segment** of the company’s journey toward its **vision**, not simply a one-year plan that ends there.”

On January 10, 2023, Simplamo experts conducted the 2023 Annual Planning session for StarGlobal 3D. Through the implementation sessions, StarGlobal was able to:

- **Remove complexity from building the goal system and help the team stay close to the 2023 plan**

With guidance from expert Nguyen Thi Nghia, the StarGlobal 3D team clarified the full picture for 2023 and **specified** quarterly priority OKR goals, while making the weekly KPI indicators each member needed to own transparent.

In addition, Starglobal 3D’s goal system was expressed clearly and closely through **milestones** on the software, guiding the team to practice “doing what is important.” Most importantly, every member now **grasped** and **understood** the strategy Mr. Hao wanted to communicate. The goals and indicators gained consensus from the StarGlobal 3D team, and members all committed to executing the company’s goals.

- **Connect the team with company goals – Review business activities through weekly meetings**

Through the implementation session, Simplamo’s guidance helped StarGlobal 3D organize an effective weekly meeting rhythm. The seven-step meeting framework helped the whole team **review** and **update the status** of the goals and indicators that had been set. This helped everyone remove the pressure of spending too much time every day on reporting through a complex process that unnecessarily affected productivity.

Simplamo helped StartGlobal clarify its annual business plan in detail. Combined with weekly meetings, this helped Mr. Hao stay close to and better understand the actual state of the business during goal execution.
