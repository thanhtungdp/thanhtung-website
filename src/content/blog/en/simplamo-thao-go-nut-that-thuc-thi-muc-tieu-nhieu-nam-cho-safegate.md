## **1. Safegate – A journey of trial and error and the first touchpoint with Simplamo**

Smart Cyber Security Joint Stock Company – [Smart Cyber Security (Safegate)](https://safegate.vn) is a cybersecurity startup built on a solid foundation, with founders who are experienced experts in information security, Cloud, Big Data, IoT, and AI.

![Safegate implements Simplamo](../../../assets/blog/simplamo-thao-go-nut-that-thuc-thi-muc-tieu-nhieu-nam-cho-safegate/Hinh-anh-casestudy-11-1024x576.png)

Safegate is a pioneering business in cybersecurity digital transformation, with a young, energetic, and ambitious team. Challenges and difficulties are unavoidable for any business. For Mr. Ngo Tuan Anh – Chairman of Safegate, the biggest challenge he faced lay in the process of **GOAL EXECUTION:**

- Business planning was spontaneous, without a clear basis for staying aligned with the actual situation of the business.
- There was **no effective method** for following execution; plans could even be forgotten after only one week.
- After the annual business plan was built, there were no people and no method to track and control it, making the annual plan difficult to complete.
- Roles and responsibilities in execution were not clearly defined; everyone worked separately and **no one stood up to take responsibility**.

![Safegate implements Simplamo](../../../assets/blog/simplamo-thao-go-nut-that-thuc-thi-muc-tieu-nhieu-nam-cho-safegate/Hinh-anh-casestudy-13-1024x576.png)

As someone who understands technology, Mr. Tuan Anh and his team spent a great deal of time researching and using many different goal management software solutions, including well-known software such as Base, but none delivered real effectiveness. Until he happened to learn about Simplamo, he decided to implement the software after only two weeks of meetings.

## 2. Simplamo – A business philosophy that breaks through barriers to goal execution

On February 22, 2023, under the guidance of Simplamo expert Nguyen Thi Nghia, the kick-off session for implementing Simplamo at Safegate took place in a lively atmosphere. The team debated together and recognized many issues within the organization.

- **Accountability chart: clarifying structure, increasing proactivity and responsibility**

**Overlapping roles and difficulties in coordinating work** were the first issues the Safegate team needed to change before moving on to 2023 business goals. Simplamo experts brought Safegate back to the most fundamental elements of a business: to create the right structure, the company needs to rely on the customer journey from arrival to departure through three basic functions: **Sales & Marketing / Operations / Finance & HR.**

Once the structure was agreed upon, the team discussed and identified the **five most important roles** for each position. Through debate came understanding. By looking back and clarifying every position on a single plane, the Safegate team realized many things they had missed during the previous period.

![Safegate builds an accountability chart on Simplamo](../../../assets/blog/simplamo-thao-go-nut-that-thuc-thi-muc-tieu-nhieu-nam-cho-safegate/Hinh-anh-Casedtudy-Le-Vy-Dang-Gia-Trang-26.png)

Simplamo expert guides Safegate on the thinking behind building an accountability chart.

- **Building the 2023 annual plan – specific and achievable**

What makes Simplamo valuable is that it turns complex things into simple things, and turns distant goals into something within reach. Simplamo experts worked with the Safegate team to clarify expectations using **the most specific numbers**. A goal must be specific, measurable, and clear enough for people to visualize what they will do; otherwise, it is only an empty statement.

With questions aimed at the right focus and by drawing out opinions from the team, Simplamo experts helped Safegate clarify the goals it wanted for 2023. Every goal gained **team consensus**, and everyone could visualize what their department would do immediately after the meeting ended.
